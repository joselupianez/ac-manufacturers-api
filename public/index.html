Air Conditioning Manufacturers

A collection of air conditioning manufacturers data including when they were founded, key people, headquarters, and brands they manufacture.

API Endpoints:

All Companies
https://ac-manufacturers.herokuapp.com/api/
Specific Company
https://ac-manufacturers.herokuapp.com/api/:name
Random Company
https://ac-manufacturers.herokuapp.com/api/random

Want to add another air conditioning manufacturer? Submit the company's information as a pull request here.
